Scientific Publication — Institute for Technology & Society (ITS Rio)

Based on my work as a Visiting Researcher at ITS Rio, this article examines how Pix and digital payments reshape everyday practices and urban governance in Rio de Janeiro, highlighting inclusion–control trade-offs and socio-spatial asymmetries.

Scientific Publication — Anthropologie & developpement (APAD)

Submitted to the peer-reviewed bilingual journal Anthropologie & developpement (APAD), this paper introduces “transactional inclusion” to analyze how instant payments reconfigure inequalities, digital dependence, traceability, and everyday negotiations of financial autonomy in Rio de Janeiro.

Digital Project — Malaysia Real-Estate Price Tracking Tool (Available soon)

Prototype platform to visualize and analyze real-estate transactions in Malaysia, combining data scraping, interactive mapping, and economic analysis to monitor market trends and explore digital transparency in property markets.

PhD Research Project — Executive Summary

Intra-urban diagnosis of economic centralities and socio-spatial disparities using strictly aggregated electronic payment data to observe microfinancial flows in the Sao Paulo Metropolitan Region (RMSP), through a reproducible, LGPD-compliant protocol.
